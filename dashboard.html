Admin Dashboard

Overview of recent activity across the wiki.

Wiki status
—

Announcement banner

Show a message at the top of every page on the site — maintenance notices, contests, anything. Visitors can dismiss it; posting a new message shows it again for everyone.

Inbox

Bug reports, suggestions, and messages sent from the “Contact the Admins” form on account pages.

Reported Conversations

When a member reports a private message conversation, it unlocks here so you can read it. Un-reported conversations stay private. After reviewing you can message either person or ban the offender from the Users panel.

Users

Ban or unban accounts, grant or remove admin, or make a one-time password-reset link to hand to a user. Bans block logging in and all editing; the user's pages stay up.

Maintenance tools

Standardize character icons → — re-fit every character's art to the 591×591 frame so icon sizes are consistent site-wide.

Unowned pages

Seeded pages have no owner account, so nobody can edit them on the site. Assign one to a user (by username) to hand it over. Leave the box empty and click Clear to remove an owner.

Deleted content

Pages deleted by their owners (or by you) are hidden from the whole site but kept here so nothing breaks by accident. Restore puts a page back exactly as it was; Purge removes it for good and cannot be undone.

Page history & rollback

Every time a page is saved, the version it replaced is kept (up to 20 per page). Pick a page to see its history and roll it back to an earlier version. The current version is saved first, so a rollback can itself be undone. The slug is the last part of the page's URL — e.g. fisherman for /c/fisherman — or use the history links in the activity log below.

Protected pages

A protected page can only be edited, published, or deleted by admins — its owner sees a “page is protected” message. Handy for finished showcase pages or anything being vandalized.

Bulk actions

Load a list of pages, tick the ones you want, then apply one action to all of them — reassign an owner, publish/unpublish, move to trash, restore, or add/remove a tag (characters only).

Broken references

Finds scripts and collections that point at characters which no longer exist (or sit in the trash / drafts), so pages don't quietly show gaps.

Orphaned images

Uploaded images that no page references any more (replaced art, purged pages, avatars of deleted accounts). Deleting them frees storage and cannot break a live page — but it can't be undone.

Backups

Nightly snapshots of the whole database (kept 30 days). Download any table as JSON, or restore a single page from a chosen night — the current version is saved to page history first, and even purged pages can be brought back this way.
