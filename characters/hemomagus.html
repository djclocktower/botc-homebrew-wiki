"Each night*, choose a player: they die. Chosen Minions swap characters with you if an Outsider lives, who then dies. [+1 Outsider]"

The Hemomagus uses the blood of Outsiders to fuel their magic.

  • If a Hemomagus attacks a Minion, they swap characters instead of the Minion dying, as long as there is a living Outsider.
  • When a swap accours, a living Outsider must die that night. Which Outsider dies is decided by the Storyteller, not the Hemomagus.
  • If the Hemomagus chooses a Minion and there are no living Outsiders, the Minion dies as usual.
  • Hemomagus swaps can happen as many times as there as living minions.
  • Deaths caused by a Hemomagus swap still count as Demon kills.

While setting up the game, take a good character token out and replace it with a blank token. When gathering tokens, replace the blank token with the one you removed in the grimoire and mark them with the CLOAKED reminder.

At any time during the game, if the Cloak publicly guesses which player is Cloaked and is incorrect, mark the Cloak with the GUESS USED reminder. Future guesses by the Cloak have no effect.

At any time during the game, if the Cloak publicly guesses which player is Cloaked and is correct, the game ends. Declare that the evil team wins.

The Cloak guesses the Ravenkeeper, who is bluffing the Soldier. They are Cloaked, and Evil wins.
The Cloak guesses the Preacher. The Preacher is not Cloaked and nothing happens.
The Saint is Cloaked and thinks they are the Recluse. They are executed, and Evil wins.
  • Sit back and observe. Does someone look suprised that a player was executed when they were nominated? They might not have known they are the Virgin.
  • Your ability can obscure Outsiders as well. Remember that when choosing who to frame or get executed.
  • Instead of looking for who is Cloaked, look for who your sure isn't. People who claim right away and aren't double claimed probably are not Cloaked.
  • Take a very close look at players that claim to be a demon bluff. They may be mistaken for what they are!
  • Try to bluff being Cloaked yourself! Play around claiming your character and be shy about sharing info. Good players might assume you are Cloaked!
  • Is nobody acting like they've been Cloaked? They might have figured out what they are quickly! Look on the script for characters that could do that and watch the players that claim to be them.
