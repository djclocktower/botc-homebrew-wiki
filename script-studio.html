Turn any script into a print-ready illustrated sheet — parchment, botanicals, colored team sections and auto-computed night order.

Script

The sheet lays itself out: team sections, adaptive columns, and the first-night / other-nights side bars computed from each character’s night order. Sheets are Teensyville-sized for now — up to 6 Townsfolk, 2 Outsiders, 2 Minions and 2 Demons; bigger scripts get multi-page sheets in a later phase.

Export

2× (3000×4200) prints nicely on A4/Letter. 3× is full 300 DPI but may fail on phones. Print PDF export is coming in a later phase.

About

This is the first release of Script Studio: the automatic renderer. Tap-to-edit — moving, resizing and restyling every element — is the next phase, followed by multi-page sheets and PDF export.
